Who says all the Bitcoin success has to go to men?

Caitlin Naidoo got started with Bitcoin in late 2017 after an unsuccessful job hunt left her family nearly broke. "I lost my job in the journalism industry and looked for months for new work with no luck. My husband worked, but we needed both incomes to pay our growing bills. I needed reliable income...fast. That's when I really started researching Bitcoin" Caitlin says.

Caitlin says her biggest fear at first was entering what seemed like a male-dominated finance and technology industry. "At first I thought, 'how am I going to make money using this? I'm just a mom who lost her job. I have no in-depth computer skills or a fancy degree in finance" Caitlin shared.

Caitlin Naidoo
